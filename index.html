About Me

My research focuses on developing and applying machine learning force fields in reaction spaces and performing uncertainty quantification within configurational spaces.

I specialize in bridging computational chemistry and machine learning, with particular interest in expansive configurational space systems and their applications to molecular dynamics simulations.

My work contributes to advancing our understanding of complex molecular systems through innovative computational approaches and data-driven methodologies.
