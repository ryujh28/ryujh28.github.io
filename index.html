Research Interests

I work at the intersection of computational chemistry and machine learning, focusing on complex systems with large configurational spaces.

  • Machine-learning interatomic potentials (MLIPs) for reactive/complex condensed-phase systems
  • Reaction network exploration in extreme environments (e.g., supercritical water)
  • Dielectric response and molecular-level characterization of liquids and solutions
  • Uncertainty quantification and robust sampling in high-dimensional configurational spaces
