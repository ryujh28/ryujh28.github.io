About Me

Hi, I’m Jae Hyu Ryu, a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Chemical and Biological Engineering at Seoul National University. The focus of my research lies in the development of machine learning interatomic potentials for systems with large and complex configuration spaces. A key area of study is reactive molecular dynamics, where the vast configuration space poses significant computational demands. Recently, my work has extended to include active learning and data reduction, aiming to enhance the efficiency of model training. Additionally, I am actively applying machine learning potentials to uncover novel physical and engineering insights that have eluded traditional classical potentials. It’s a pleasure to meet you!
